# NutriScan User Guide

## Getting Started

NutriScan is a mobile nutrition tracking app that helps you scan food products, track your daily intake, and receive AI-powered recipe suggestions based on your dietary preferences.

## Installation

### For iOS/Android (Development)

1. Install the **Expo Go** app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Scan the QR code provided by your development server
3. The app will load in Expo Go

### Requirements

- iOS 13.0+ or Android 8.0+
- Camera access for barcode scanning
- Internet connection for syncing data

---

## Creating Your Account

### Registration

1. Open NutriScan
2. Tap **"Create Account"**
3. Enter a username (must be unique)
4. Create a password
5. Tap **"Register"**

### Login

1. Open NutriScan
2. Enter your username and password
3. Tap **"Sign In"**

---

## Onboarding

After creating your account, you'll complete a 6-step onboarding process to personalize your experience.

### Step 1: Welcome

A brief introduction to NutriScan's features.

### Step 2: Allergies

Add any food allergies you have:
1. Tap **"Add Allergy"**
2. Enter the allergy name (e.g., "peanuts", "shellfish")
3. Select severity:
   - **Mild** - Minor discomfort
   - **Moderate** - Significant reaction
   - **Severe** - Life-threatening
4. Tap **"Add"**
5. Repeat for additional allergies
6. Tap **"Continue"**

### Step 3: Health Conditions

Select any health conditions that affect your diet:
- Diabetes
- High Blood Pressure
- Heart Disease
- Celiac Disease
- IBS
- Kidney Disease

Tap conditions to select/deselect, then **"Continue"**.

### Step 4: Diet Type

Choose your primary diet:
- **Omnivore** - Eats all foods
- **Vegetarian** - No meat
- **Vegan** - No animal products
- **Pescatarian** - Fish but no meat
- **Keto** - Low carb, high fat
- **Paleo** - Whole foods only
- **Gluten-Free** - No gluten

### Step 5: Goals

Select your primary health goal:
- **Lose Weight** - Calorie deficit
- **Gain Muscle** - Protein focus
- **Maintain Weight** - Balanced intake
- **Eat Healthier** - General wellness

### Step 6: Preferences

Configure your lifestyle preferences:

**Activity Level:**
- Sedentary (little to no exercise)
- Light (1-3 days/week)
- Moderate (3-5 days/week)
- Active (6-7 days/week)
- Athlete (intense training)

**Cooking Skill:**
- Beginner
- Intermediate
- Advanced

**Cooking Time:**
- Quick (< 15 min)
- Moderate (15-30 min)
- Extended (30-60 min)
- Leisurely (> 60 min)

**Household Size:** Number of people you cook for

**Cuisine Preferences:** Select cuisines you enjoy (Italian, Mexican, Asian, etc.)

Tap **"Complete Setup"** to finish.

## Scanning Food

### Using the Camera

1. Tap the **Camera** tab
2. Point your camera at a barcode
3. Hold steady - the app will automatically detect barcodes
4. Once scanned, you'll see the nutrition details

### Supported Barcodes

- EAN-13 (standard groceries)
- EAN-8 (small products)
- UPC-A (US products)
- UPC-E (compact UPC)
- QR Codes

### Taking Photos

For products without barcodes:
1. Tap the **shutter button** (white circle)
2. Frame the nutrition label
3. Take the photo
4. Manually enter nutrition information

### Using Flash

Tap the **lightning bolt** icon to toggle the flashlight for low-light scanning.

### From Gallery

Tap the **gallery icon** to select an image from your photo library.

## Nutrition Details

After scanning or selecting a product, you'll see:

### Product Information
- Product name and brand
- Product image (if available)
- When you scanned it

### Nutrition Facts
- **Calories** - Highlighted prominently
- **Protein** - Grams per serving
- **Carbs** - Grams per serving
- **Fat** - Grams per serving
- Additional: Fiber, Sugar, Sodium

### AI Suggestions

Based on the scanned item and your dietary preferences, you'll get 4 personalized suggestions:

1. **Recipe #1** - A dish using the ingredient
   - Difficulty level (Easy/Medium/Hard)
   - Time estimate

2. **Recipe #2** - Another recipe option
   - Tailored to your cooking skill
   - Considers your time availability

3. **Kid Activity** - Fun craft or activity
   - Often uses packaging creatively
   - Family-friendly projects

4. **Food Pairing** - Complementary items
   - What goes well with this food
   - Drink suggestions

## Tips for Best Results

### Scanning Tips

1. **Good lighting** - Ensure adequate light on the barcode
2. **Steady hand** - Hold the phone still
3. **Clean lens** - Wipe your camera lens
4. **Flat surface** - Flatten rounded products
5. **Distance** - Hold 4-8 inches from barcode

### Accurate Tracking

1. Scan items **when you consume them**
2. Check portion sizes match your intake
3. Review your daily summary regularly
4. Update your goals as they change

### Getting Better Suggestions

1. Complete your dietary profile fully
2. Add all allergies and intolerances
3. Be accurate about cooking skill
4. Update preferences if they change

## Troubleshooting

### Barcode Won't Scan

1. Ensure camera permission is granted
2. Check lighting conditions
3. Clean your camera lens
4. Try different angles
5. Make sure barcode isn't damaged

### App Won't Load

1. Check internet connection
2. Force close and reopen the app
3. Make sure you're on the latest version

### Login Issues

1. Verify username is correct
2. Check password (case-sensitive)
3. Try registering a new account

### Data Not Syncing

1. Check internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh
3. Sign out and back in

### Suggestions Not Loading

1. Wait a moment - AI processing takes time
2. Check internet connection
3. Try again later if service is busy
